Quality in education access to education is important, but just as important is actually learning basic knowledge and skills in the classroom. Many children across the world cannot read, despite of having attended school for several years.


Insufficient focus on quality and learning contributed to what is now referred to as a global learning crisis. A greater number of children than ever before attend school today but many do not learn basic skills during their schooling.
